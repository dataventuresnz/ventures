# Used car price benchmarking

Used car buyers and sellers don't have easy access to benchmarking tools which allow them to set/pay a fair price.

**Concept** 

Take the guesswork out of buying and selling cars.

We’d love to hear from you - please get hold of us if you’re interested, or have any questions or comments, at dataventures [at] stats.govt.nz.
